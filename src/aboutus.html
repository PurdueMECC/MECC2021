About Us

Our mission

To design and develop a marine energy desalination system unrivaled in its capacity to provide clean, affordable, and accessible water to coastal communities.

Target Customers:

  • Remote developing island states that currently rely on expensive imported fuel to power desalination plants.
  • Small island resorts balancing growing tourism with insufficient local water supply
  • Non-profit organizations aiming to conserve the marine ecosystem by promoting sustainable measures
